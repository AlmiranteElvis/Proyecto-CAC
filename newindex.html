AstraVoyage?

When the expected is the unexpected

Our tourist space flights provide a unique and unforgettable experience for our clients, we offer suborbital and orbital flights aboard spacecraft specially designed for space tourism. Our clients can enjoy breathtaking views of Earth from space, experience weightlessness, and participate in exciting activities such as stargazing and astrophotography. In addition, we offer a personalized service to make sure that our clients have an unforgettable experience.

On the other hand, we are also a company dedicated to agricultural research and development in space. Our team of experts works on the development of new crops to be grown in space, taking advantage of the unique conditions of the space environment to improve the quality and quantity of crops. We also research new methods and technologies to improve the efficiency and sustainability of agriculture on Earth.

In short, our company combines the excitement and adventure of space tourism with the research and development of new agricultural solutions. We are committed to innovating and advancing space technology to make space accessible and beneficial for all.
